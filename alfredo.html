Alfredo

This is a classic Fettucine Alfredo recipe, that is easily customizable and delicious on its own. Adding your choice of protein makes this a hearty meal that'll be constantly requested.

Ingredients
  • 24 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup butter
  • ¾ pint heavy cream
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 dash garlic salt
  • ¾ cup grated Romano cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
